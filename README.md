# Decision-Engine
Idea: A web-based system that helps users analyze life decisions by comparing short-term and long-term consequences of different choices.  Problem it solves: People often make impulsive decisions without thinking about future impact.  Solution: This tool simulates outcomes of different choices and shows their impact.
A Life Decision Simulation System that helps users understand the real consequences of their choices before they make them.

🚀 Overview

Decision Engine is a futuristic web-based tool designed to simulate the outcomes of everyday life decisions.
Instead of guessing, users can visually compare how different choices affect their short-term comfort and long-term growth.

This project is built with a simple idea:

“Every decision has a future — this tool shows it.”

🎯 Problem Statement

Most people make decisions based on:

Instant gratification
Emotion in the moment
Lack of future perspective

This often leads to:

Poor productivity
Broken discipline
Missed opportunities
💡 Solution

Decision Engine solves this by:

Simulating multiple outcomes for each choice
Showing time-based consequences (1 hour → 1 month)
Highlighting impact on discipline, focus, and growth
Encouraging logical thinking over impulsive behavior
⚙️ Features
🧠 Dual Choice Analyzer

Compare two decisions side-by-side:

Option A vs Option B
Clear outcome breakdown
⏳ Time-Based Simulation

Each decision is analyzed across:

Immediate impact (1 hour)
Short-term impact (1 day)
Medium-term impact (1 week)
Long-term impact (1 month)
📊 Impact Scoring System

Every choice affects:

Focus Score
Discipline Score
Productivity Level
Long-term Growth Indicator
⚖️ Better Choice Detection

Automatically highlights:

Which option has better long-term benefits
Why it is better (simple reasoning)
🔥 Reality Feedback System

No motivation, no quotes — only truth:

“This choice improves short-term comfort but reduces long-term discipline.”

🕓 Decision History
Stores previous decisions
Helps users analyze their behavior patterns over time
